Our DevSecOps Coaching Services

Our experienced DevSecOps coaches collaborate with your teams, guiding them to optimise their processes and technologies. The goal is simple: to deliver value faster safer, without compromising on quality.

Culture: In DevSecOps, success isn't just about tools and processes. "Culture eats strategy for breakfast". DevSecOps thrives only where the culture is aligned with the processes and technology. In organisations with high DevSecOps maturity there is focus on things like: Psychological Safety , Intent Based Leadership, Generative Culture, Team Topologies, Project to Product and VUCA.

People and mindset: DevSecOps is not just a set of practices; it's a mindset that transforms how teams approach their work. Our coaches dive deep into your existing processes, identify bottlenecks, and implement streamlined workflows. The result? Enhanced efficiency, reduced lead times, stay safe and an accelerated path to value.

Technology ecosystem: Our DevSecOps coaching extends to fit-for-purpose tools and technologies, ensuring your teams are using resources to deliver value, stay safe and drive innovation.

What do you get?

Experience and Expertise Our DevSecOps coaches bring years of hands-on relevant experience and they have worked in several industries and have skills in multiple areas, such as Development, Cloud, Infrastructure, Networking, Security, Service Management and leadership.

Coaching tailored to your needs Every organisation or team is different and needs different outcomes and a different coaching approach. Engagements typically vary from 2 to 5 days per week and from 6 months to 2 years.
