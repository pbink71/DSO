Master your DevSecOps journey with our maturity assessment.

Struggling to bridge the gap between development and operations? Our DevOps maturity assessment delves deep into the key areas: Development & Testing, Release & Deployment, Monitoring & Optimisation, and Culture, organisation & people. We go beyond generic assessments, focusing on your specific requirements and goals.

Approach Through rigorous analysis, we'll illuminate your strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities across each phase. We'll translate findings into a tailored roadmap, guiding you towards faster deployments, fewer errors, and a thriving DevSecOps culture.

Every assessment is fully tailored to your needs and progress is shared continuously during the assessment, so adjustment is possible if required.


Focus areas:
  • Development & Testing: assess coding practices, continuous integration, and testing practices.
  • Release & deployment: Evaluate effective use of test environments and deployment into Production.
  • Monitoring & optimisation: Analyse the monitoring of infrastructure, network, database, application, and optimisation processes.
  • Culture, organisation & people: Identify the culture, innovation, organisational processes, and people needs.

What do you get?

Assessment outcome is a detailed analysis of the current state of your DevOps practices, identifying strengths, uncovering critical gaps, and benchmarking your processes against industry best practices.

Strategic roadmap that documents your suggested activities for the short, medium and long term. A maturity assessment typically takes 2 months.
