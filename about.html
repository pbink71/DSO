About Us

DevSecOps Solutions is a small Kiwi company that focusses to help organiations to get better in DevSecOps. In 2023 Peter Bink founded DevSecOps Solutions.

Peter Bink

He started his career in IT in 1995 as a developer at Capgemini in the Netherlands. Initially he developed in Cobol, Pascal and C. After that he performed every role in the SDLC, and with focus on Configuration & Release Management, testing and Test Management, Process Improvement in both Dev and Ops. Throughout his career he was doing DevOps without knowing he was doing it, such as writing a script to compile a new build, destroy the old test environment and deploy the new build for testing. In 2008 he moved to Australia and in 2012 to New Zealand.
